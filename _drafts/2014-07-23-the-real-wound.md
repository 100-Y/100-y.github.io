当伤口被辣椒染红，血淋淋的正是辣椒；当伤口被蒜泥裹烫，热乎乎的也正是蒜泥。

- When wounds are dyed red by chili, it is the chili that’s bloodily vivid; when wounds are scalded by garlic paste, it is the garlic that’s fervently hot.
