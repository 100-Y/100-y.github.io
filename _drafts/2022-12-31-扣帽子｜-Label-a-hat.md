哪怕是天上够不着的星星，弱者都会给它扣上一顶灾难的帽子。这是，极其不自信的表现。

\- Even if it is a star that cannot be reached in the sky, the weak will label it as a hat of catastrophe. This is an extremely unconfident expression.
