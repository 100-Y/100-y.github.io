最丑陋的天，是阴天  
灰色的云层，像一叠厚厚的棉  
只一条没有尽头的路  
隔着无数死寂的荒原

\- The ugliest day, is a cloudy day  
\- Gray clouds, like a stack of thick cotton  
\- Only one road with no end  
\- Separated by countless dead wastelands
