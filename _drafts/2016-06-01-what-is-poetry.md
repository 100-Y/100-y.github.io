诗歌……  
如遇外力变化；  
内心极度抗拒，  
而深藏功与名；  
不求思索。  
诗歌……  
就不是诗歌。

- Poetry…
- Changes in case of external force;
- And the heart is extremely resistant,
- Hide merit and fame deeply;
- Without thinking.
- Poetry…
- It is not poetry.
