山的那边，有几朵大的白云……  
这边一朵——乌云；  
几丝凉风、几滴窗雨。  
白云连成一片……  
大风吹，  
大雨来了。

- On other side of mountain, there are several large white clouds…
- On this side, there is one—dark cloud;
- A few strands of cool breeze, a few drops of window rain.
- The white clouds are connected together…
- Strong wind blows,
- Heavy rain comes.
