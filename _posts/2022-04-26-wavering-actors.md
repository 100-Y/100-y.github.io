台上唱戏的戏子，左顾右盼、犹豫不决，需要及时下台，如果是继续唱下去，便注定贻笑大方。

- The actors on stage, looking around and hesitating, need to get off the stage in time, if they continue to sing, they are bound to make a fool of themselves.
