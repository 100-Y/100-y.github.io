一些人  
一时被内心深处的  
一些种子带动起来了，就像  
一个时候  
一个阶段的  
一些ta们。曾经ta们中的  
一些，有血有肉；曾经ta们中的  
一些，成为了时代的炮灰。

- Some people have been motivated by
- Some seeds deep in their hearts for a
- Some while, just like
- Some of them
- Some time at a
- Some stage. once
- Some of them, have flesh and blood; once
- Some of them, became the cannon-fodder of the time.
