昨夜，少年丢失了灵魂，  
花开雪白如玫瑰地红。  
若太阳明早重新从东方升起，  
今日，湖水会再次浸染夕阳。

- Last night, the teenager had lost his soul,
- Blooming flowers were snow-white as bloody roses.
- If sun rises tomorrow morning in east again,
- Today, the sunset will fresh soak up into lake-water.
