远上碧空啸云天，  
俯视山野赶牦牛。  
但愿晴时雨里任，  
不肯泥淖水中游。

- Far above blue sky howling,
- Looking down mountains and chasing yaks.
- May it be rainy or sunny flying,
- Not to swim in muddy water.
