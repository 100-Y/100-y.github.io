放弃毁灭一切，  
在它们“杀死”你以前；  
纵使血肉模糊，亦满怀感恩的心。  
再从旁观者的嘴里，  
与眼神中读得：  
将他们杀死，  
先用“爱心”和“宽容”；  
在它们杀死你以前。

- Give up destroying everything,
- Before they “kill” you;
- Even though the bloody-flesh is blurred, be full of gratitude.
- And then from the-mouth of the bystander,
- And the-eyes to read:
- Kill them,
- First use “love” and “forgiveness”
- Before they kill you.
