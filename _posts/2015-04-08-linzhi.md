树上盘着有蛇么，  
我还准备在树下睡觉。  
不要吓我，  
一个人！

林芝

- Is there a snake coiled in the tree?
- Yet, i’m preparing to sleep underneath.
- Do not frighten me,
- One alone!

- Linzhi
