日月为羁绊，  
天地作铺床。  
来时勤做米，  
去亦了为然。  
唤尔遵义否？  
道我是浪仙。

- The sun and the moon are the shackles,
- The heaven and the earth are the beds.
- When you come, work hard for me,
- When you go, you will go.
- Do you obey me?
- Saying that I am a wandering immortal.
