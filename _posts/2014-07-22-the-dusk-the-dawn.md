请求远离我吧，  
腐小的残埃 / 内心龌龊的人儿；  
趁那黄昏 / 黎明  
还未来临 / 来临了啊，  
荡涤我的灵魂。

- Please stay away from me,
- You rotten residual ashes / You people hearts with dirty;
- Before the dusk / the dawn
- Has yet to come / come,
- Purge my soul.
