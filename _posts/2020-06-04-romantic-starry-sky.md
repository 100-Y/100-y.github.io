星空下，最浪漫的是诗！  
星空里，则是你和我。

站立于这同一片星空下，  
愿你我都可以拥有浪漫诗歌一般的归宿！  
照亮他人，  
化作星空里众多闪烁星中的一颗。

- Under starry sky, the most romantic thing is poetry!
- In, it is you and me.

- Standing under this same starry sky,
- Hope you and I can have a romantic poetic home!
- Illuminate others,
- And become one of the many twinkling stars in.
