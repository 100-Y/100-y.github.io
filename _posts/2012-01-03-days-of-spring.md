东风吹斜柳，  
晴雨春来归。  
小楼逐日暖，  
独燕蝶双飞。

- The east wind brushes willows sway,
- Spring returns with sun and showers.
- Small pavilion warms each passing day,
- Alone the swallow and butterfly dance in pairs.
