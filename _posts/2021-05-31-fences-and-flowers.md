睁眼不见周围的藩篱、栅栏和鲜花，盲目一味地唱赞歌或是抹黑、抨击，都是不负责任的做法。

- It is irresponsible, both to open eyes without seeing the fences and flowers around, and blindly to sing hymns or to criticize.
