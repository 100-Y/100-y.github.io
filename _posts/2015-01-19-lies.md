无数个白昼黑夜，  
我都幻想身处在一片荒野。  
肆意奔跑、滚躺，  
累了傻笑、哭泣。  
两人也好，  
自己一人  
也罢。

- Countless days and nights,
- I’ve dreamt of being in a wilderness.
- Run and roll freewheeling,
- Giggle and cry when tired.
- Two people are fine,
- One by myself
- Is fine too.
